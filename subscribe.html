Frequently asked questions

How does access work right now?
Register with your chosen plan — your account is created instantly in our system. For Starter and Pro, the admin will manually activate your access within 24 hours while Stripe automated billing is being set up.
How does the auto-trade work?
Pro subscribers download BLEETrader, connect their own Schwab or IBKR API keys, and every day at 3:52 PM ET the app automatically reads the BLEE signal and places rebalance orders in their own brokerage account. You stay in full control.
Is my brokerage account safe?
Yes. Your API keys are stored only on your own computer. BLEE never has access to your brokerage account or funds. You can revoke access at any time from your Schwab or IBKR developer settings.
Can I upgrade from Starter to Pro later?
Yes — contact admin or use the member portal to upgrade. Upgrades are reflected immediately in your account once confirmed.
